1.

Information We Collect

ElectroTrical collects information necessary to provide professional PCB layout, design, and manufacturing services. Here's what we gather and why:

Personal Information

  • Contact Details: Your name, email address, and phone number (provided when you request a quote or create an account)
  • Company Information: Company name, industry, and business details to understand your project scope
  • Project Information: Technical specifications, PCB requirements, design files, schematics, and project documentation
  • Manufacturing Specifications: Gerber files, board dimensions, layer count, copper weight, surface finish, solder mask color, via covering options, and other PCB specifications submitted via our online manufacturing quote configurator

Payment Information

  • Billing Details: We do not store your full credit card information. Payment processing is handled securely by Stripe
  • Invoice Records: We maintain transaction records for accounting and service delivery purposes

Technical Information

  • Server Logs: IP addresses, browser type, referring pages, and access times
  • Cookies & Tracking: Session data and analytics information (see Section 5)
  • Device Information: Operating system, browser version, and device type

Important: Your project files are treated with the highest confidentiality. We use industry-standard encryption to protect all uploaded documents and designs.

2.

How We Use Information

We use collected information exclusively for purposes related to providing PCB design and manufacturing services and improving our platform:

Information Type Primary Use
Name & Email Service delivery, communication, project updates
Project Files & Specs PCB layout design and technical analysis
Gerber Files & Manufacturing Specs PCB manufacturing quote generation and order processing. Specs are shared with our manufacturing partners solely to fulfil your order.
Payment Information Invoice processing and order payment management
Server Logs & IP Security monitoring, fraud prevention, system maintenance
Analytics Data Website performance optimization and user experience improvement

What We Don't Do

  • We never sell or rent your personal information to third parties
  • We do not use your data for marketing purposes without explicit consent
  • We do not share your project files with competitors or unauthorized parties
  • We do not use your information for behavioral advertising or profiling

Confidentiality: All project information you share remains confidential and is protected under our client agreements.

3.

Data Security

ElectroTrical implements comprehensive security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

Encryption & Transport Security

  • SSL/TLS 256-bit Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard TLS 1.2 or higher
  • HTTPS Protocol: Our entire platform uses HTTPS to ensure secure communication
  • Data in Transit: Project files are encrypted during upload and download operations

Payment Security

  • PCI DSS Compliance: Payment processing is handled by Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified
  • No Direct Storage: We never store full credit card numbers on our servers
  • Tokenization: Payment details are tokenized for secure, recurring transactions

Infrastructure Security

  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Firewalls and DDoS protection
  • Regular software updates and security patches
  • Secure access controls and authentication
  • Automated backup systems for data recovery

Security Standards: While we implement comprehensive security measures, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication where available.

4.

Third-Party Services

ElectroTrical uses carefully selected third-party services to enhance functionality and security. We only share necessary data and maintain data processing agreements with all vendors:

Payment Processing

  • Stripe: Handles all payment processing. Your payment data is processed according to Stripe's Privacy Policy

PCB Manufacturing Partners

  • Manufacturing Facilities: When you place a PCB manufacturing order, we share only the technical specifications necessary to produce your boards (Gerber files, layer count, dimensions, material specs). Our manufacturing partners are contractually bound to use your data solely for production purposes and may not retain or re-use it.

Front-End Libraries & Resources

Bot Protection & Security

  • reCAPTCHA: Used on forms to prevent spam and abuse. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

Data Processing

All third-party service providers are contractually obligated to:

  • Process data only as instructed by ElectroTrical
  • Maintain appropriate security measures
  • Implement data minimization principles
  • Comply with applicable data protection regulations

Your Control: You can control certain third-party cookies through your browser settings or our cookie preferences (see Section 5).

5.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, maintain session security, and analyze platform performance:

Types of Cookies

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Session Cookies Maintain login state and user preferences Session ends or 30 days
Security Cookies CSRF protection, fraud prevention Session dependent
Analytics Cookies Track website usage and performance Up to 2 years
Third-Party Cookies Stripe payments, Google services Per vendor policy

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome/Edge: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data

Note that disabling cookies may affect functionality such as login persistence and form submission.

Analytics & Performance

We monitor website performance metrics including page load times, user flow, and error rates. This data helps us optimize the platform but does not identify individual users.

Do Not Track: We respect browser Do Not Track (DNT) signals where applicable. If you enable DNT, we will not use analytics tracking cookies.

6.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes:

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Account Information Active account + 2 years Service continuity, legal compliance
Project & Design Files Contract period + 3 years Service delivery, dispute resolution
Gerber & Manufacturing Files Order completion + 2 years Re-order support, quality assurance, dispute resolution

Deletion Upon Request

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (subject to legal retention requirements). Contact info@electrotrical.com with deletion requests.

Legal Hold: If you're involved in any legal proceedings, we may retain data longer than stated above to comply with legal obligations or court orders.

7.

Your Rights

We respect your privacy rights and comply with applicable data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA. You have the following rights:

Right to Access

You have the right to request and receive a copy of all personal data we hold about you in a portable, machine-readable format.

Right to Correction

You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information in your account at any time.

Right to Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements and our ability to continue providing services.

Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format to transfer to another service provider.

Right to Object

You can object to processing of your personal data for non-essential purposes such as marketing or profiling.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us with your request:

  • Email: info@electrotrical.com
  • Subject Line: Data Rights Request [Access/Deletion/Correction/Portability]

We will respond to your request within 30 days. We may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request.

No Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights. Your ability to use our services will not be affected by your data requests.

8.

Changes to This Policy

ElectroTrical may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website with a new "Last updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification (if the change significantly affects your privacy rights)
  • Requiring explicit consent for certain material changes

Your continued use of ElectroTrical's services after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
