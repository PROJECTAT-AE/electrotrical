You need all copper layers (GTL, GBL, G2L, G3L...), solder mask layers (GTS, GBS), silkscreen layers (GTO, GBO), board outline (GKO or GM1), and drill file (XLN or DRL). Compress everything into a ZIP. Our system auto-detects layers and shows a 3D preview.
Price depends on board dimensions, layer count, quantity, surface finish, copper weight, and lead time. Larger boards, more layers, and special finishes like ENIG increase cost. The instant quote calculator gives you a real-time estimate.
For standard FR-4, minimum trace/space is 3/3 mil (0.075mm). For HDI boards using laser drilling, we can achieve 2.5/2.5 mil. We recommend 4/4 mil or wider for reliable production. Our free DFM check flags any violations automatically.
Yes. Our turnkey PCBA handles PCB fabrication, component sourcing (500K+ parts), SMT/THT soldering, AOI, and optional X-ray. Upload your BOM and Gerber together for a combined quote. We support BGAs, QFNs, and 0201 passives.
We offer HASL (Lead-free), ENIG, OSP, Hard Gold, Immersion Silver, and Immersion Tin. HASL is most economical. ENIG provides the best flat surface for fine-pitch SMD. Hard Gold is best for connectors and fingers.
Standard 2-layer boards build in 24 hours. 4-layer takes 3–4 days; 6+ layers take 4–7 days. DHL Express ships most orders globally in 2–5 days. Rush processing available for additional fee.
Yes, our DFM system automatically reviews your design for trace width, spacing, via placement, and more. Any issues are flagged before manufacturing, helping ensure quality and first-pass success.
We ship to 180+ countries with full tracking. Standard shipping via DHL Express typically takes 2–5 business days. Customs and import duties vary by destination — check with your local customs before ordering.
