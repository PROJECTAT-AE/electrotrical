Our Services

Everything Your Board Needs

End-to-end PCB design services for RF, high-speed digital, power, mixed-signal, and flex circuits — any complexity, any scale.

PCB Layout & Routing

Complete PCB layout from schematic to production-ready Gerbers. Multi-layer routing with optimized signal integrity and thermal management.

Schematic Capture

Professional schematic design and capture. Clean, hierarchical documentation with proper symbol libraries and design rule validation.

Design Review & DFM

Comprehensive design review for manufacturability, signal integrity, and thermal performance. Catch issues before fabrication.

RF & Microwave Design

Specialized RF layouts with microstrip, coplanar waveguide, antenna placement, ground pours, shielding, and EMI mitigation strategies.

High-Speed Digital

DDR4/5, PCIe 4.0, USB 3.2, Ethernet — controlled impedance, differential pair routing, and optimized layer stackup design.

Flex & Rigid-Flex

Single and multi-layer flex circuits with correct bend radius, dynamic flex zones, and coverlay design for demanding applications.

Portfolio

Recent Projects

A selection of boards we have designed and delivered for clients across industries.

Embedded Systems · Space

CubeSat On-Board Computer (OBC)

8-layer OBC for a 3U CubeSat built around a radiation-tolerant ARM Cortex-R5 processor with 256 MB NOR flash, 8 MB SRAM, SpaceWire interface, CAN 2.0B bus, hardware watchdog, and TCXO-disciplined RTC. PC-104 form factor, ECSS-E-ST-40C compliant.

RF Design · Space

S-Band Satellite Transceiver

4-layer RF PCB for CubeSat S-band uplink/downlink (2.025–2.29 GHz) with integrated LNA (NF 0.8 dB), +30 dBm PA, TCXO reference, and impedance-matched microstrip feed to an on-board patch antenna. Meets ITU space radiocommunication requirements.

High-Speed Digital · Space

Star Tracker Image Sensor Board

8-layer high-density board pairing a 4 MP CMOS star tracker sensor with an FPGA attitude processor. Features LVDS differential data pairs, precision 40 MHz clock distribution, radiation-tolerant SRAM, and thermal management designed for LEO thermal cycling (−40 °C to +85 °C).
